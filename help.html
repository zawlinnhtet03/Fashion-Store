ORDER STATUS CHANGE

We make every attempt to ensure that items you have ordered are available.
However, occasionally we run out of popular items.
If you ordered an item that is out of stock, we will send you an email as soon as possible to let you know.
If we do not expect to receive your ordered item back in stock, we will cancel that item from your order.
If you paid with a credit card, you will not be charged for the canceled item, or for taxes or shipping on that item.
